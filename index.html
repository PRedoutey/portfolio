About Me

My first exposure to programming was in high school, where I helped manage the school website. It sparked my interest in programming, which led me to pursue it at Shawnee State University, where I completed my Bachelors in Digital Simulation & Gaming with a minor in Computer Engineering Technology. I maintained a 4.0 GPA while also being a member of Shawnee State's esports team.

I am currently working as an intern at Yost Labs, Inc. in Portsmouth, OH.

Escape from the Labyrinth

Escape from the Labyrinth was the first game I created. I made it in pygame toward the end of my first year of college. The goal is to use pressure plates, levers, and portals to reach the end of the levels.

It was originally a group project, but my partner dropped the class after we finished planning the features of the game. Even with the additional work, I was happy at how it turned out.
